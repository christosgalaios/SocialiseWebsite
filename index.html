What we do

Everything you need
to get out there.

Discover Events.

Browse a curated feed of local happenings, from cultural nights and live music to sports and chill hangouts. Filter by vibe, not just category.

Micro-Meets.

Get matched into small group hangouts of 4–6 people who share your interests. No awkwardness, just good food and great conversation.

Communities.

Join groups built around the things you love. Share moments, plan meetups, and stay connected with people who get you.

Meet MangoAI.

Your personal assistant inside Socialise. MangoAI helps you find events, suggests people to meet, and keeps your social life effortlessly on track.
