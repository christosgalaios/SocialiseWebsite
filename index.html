Features

Everything you need to socialise.

From discovering events to building lasting friendships, we've got you covered.

Discover Events

Explore pub nights, hikes, board games, social deduction, speed friendships, and more. There's always something wholesome happening near you.

Micro-Meets

Wholesome small-group hangouts of 4-6 people at curated venues. No awkward silences — just great vibes and new mates.

Communities

Join Tribes that match your passions. Whether you're into hiking, board games, or book clubs, find your people and keep connecting.

MangoAI

Your personal AI assistant. Just say "I fancy a walk this weekend" and Mango finds the perfect event. It learns your interests and gets smarter over time.

Inclusivity Built In

Tag events with #DogsWelcome, #WheelchairAccessible, #LGBTQ+, and more. Everyone deserves to feel welcome — we make sure they do.

Level Up

Earn XP by attending events, hosting hangouts, and being a great community member. Unlock titles and grow your social confidence.
